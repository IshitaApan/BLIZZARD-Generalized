[Progress] Color and Cursor leaks in ProgressInfoItem

3.2M3 Sleak shows Color and Cursor leaks in ProgressInfoItem. Steps to reproduce: - newly installed 3.2M3 with sleak (http://www.eclipse.org/swt/tools.php) - open progress and sleak views - enable 'show sleeping and system tasks' -> each 'remove all finished tasks' now leaks 4 Colors and 4 Cursors The leaks also show up with the build job, in that case the leak is about 17 Colors and 14 Cursors for each auto-build with sleeping and system tasks shown.
