E4Application does not configure Display.setAppName()

Created attachment 189142 patch to configure the app name in E4Application#getApplicationDisplay() E3.x's org.eclipse.ui.internal.Workbench#createDisplay() calls o.e.swt.widgets.Display.setAppName() with the product's app name. E4Application (or something) should too. The attached patch sets the display name as part of E4Application#getApplicationDisplay().
