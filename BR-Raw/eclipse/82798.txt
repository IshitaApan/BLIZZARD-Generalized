TableViewer internalRefresh is very slow as of 1.19

This performance problem was introduced with ver 1.19 of TableViewer (fix for 72794). I use a TableViewer with >1000 rows and 8+ columns. Sorting on a column using a ViewerSorter and tableViewer.refresh() now takes > 10 sec where before it took < 1 sec. The fix in ver 1.19 of TableViewer changed a line in TableViewer.internalRefresh(Object, boolean) from this: items[i].setImage(new Image[0]); to this: items[i].setImage(new Image[table.getItemCount()]);//Clear all images For my table, the above line will allocate and call setImage with an array of > 1000 null images. It should be getColumnCount() instead of getItemCount(). I believe the following will correctly fix 72794 without causing performance problems: items[i].setImage(new Image[table.getColumnCount()]);//Clear all images
