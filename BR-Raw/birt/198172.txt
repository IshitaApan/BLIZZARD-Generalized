Crosstab - cannot cast from BigDecimal to Double

Build ID: I20070621-1340 Steps To Reproduce: I created a SQL data set on Oracle 9 with a text column and two decimal columns. I then created a cube with the text column as one group, a decimal column as the second group, and the sum of the other decimal column as the measurement. When I then build a crosstab, and preview it, I get the error "java.math.BigDecimal cannot be cast to java.lang.Double." More information: I just found a related bug 155138.
